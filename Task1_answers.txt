Task 1 — Turtlesim discovery Task 1 answers

1) What is the name of the publisher node?
 teleop_turtle
 if you run one of our nodes (straight_line, circle, roomba, goto, etc.) that node will be the publisher for /turtle1/cmd_vel while it runs.

2)What is the  Message type of cmd_vel?
Type: geometry_msgs/msg/Twist
Fields:
Vector3 linear
  float64 x
  float64 y
  float64 z
Vector3 angular
  float64 x
  float64 y
  float64 z

3) What is the  frequency of publication f the node publishing on  /cmd_vel?
10 Hz (e.g., average rate readings: ~9.93–10.00 Hz).

4) What is the Message type of /turtle1/pose:
Type: turtlesim/msg/Pose
Fields:
float32 x
float32 y
float32 theta
float32 linear_velocity
float32 angular_velocity
